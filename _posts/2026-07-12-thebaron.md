This is my second time reading this novel.  I first read it over twenty five years.  I picked up a French copy at a used booked store when I was in Europe.  So it's been a while since I've read it.  

This time I had an English copy that I read to my son.  It didn't always keep his attention rapt but there is one scene in the book where he had a visceral reaction.  After the bandit Gian dei Brughi becomes addicted to books he no longer wants to be a bandit any longer.  Two other bandits blackmail him into doing another dirty deed by stealing a book and ripping pages out of it.  Every time a page was ripped from the book in the story my son screamed in agony.  You would have thought that I was ripping out the last pages in the book that we were reading.  This is why I keep coming back to Italo Calvino novels, he was a master storyteller.
